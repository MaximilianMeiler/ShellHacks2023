Course Description

Credits: 1; This course covers basic pedagogy, especially as it relates to computer science and engineering. We will cover three fundamental elements in education – learning environment, educational theory, and educational practice – as well as approached to engineering-specific training. Topics include how into interact effectively with students, models of learning and expertise development, and how to implement approaches in a classroom.
